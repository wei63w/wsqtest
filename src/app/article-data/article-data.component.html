Functional Determinism and Non-determinism
1.functionally Determinism
所有可能的不同的input 都会得到一个相同的output

2.functionally non-determinism
同一个input 在不同的场合会得到不同的output
**operationally deterministic**
对每一个可能的input 都用same sequence of steps
##note
Any operationally deterministic algorithm must also be functionally
deterministic, but the converse does not hold.
